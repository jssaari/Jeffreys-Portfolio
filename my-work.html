Innovative Home Care Family Vets

Highlighted Project

Visit Site
  • Innovative Home Care Family Vets is an organization in Southern California that provides home care for the families of deployed Veterans. I love their mission and enjoyed working with their ever-joyous CEO Jason Bonney. The theme of the IHCF Vets website is made completely from scratch using the languages below while the backend is powered by WordPress with a custom CMS. Jason wanted to make it clear through web design that the mission of IHCF Vets is to support families by using patriotic colors and displaying pictures/videos of veterans with their families. In our first couple meetings, I presented Jason with mock up designs and we would share ideas with one another. With our combined vision and my expertise in web design we created IHCFVets.org.
