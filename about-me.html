Hi, I'm Jeffrey.

My journey into full-stack engineering began while I was in pursuit of my bachelor's degree in computer science. As a full-time student with little-to-no marketable skills at the time, I decided to dedicate all of my free time to learning web development. In less than one year, I began my professional career at a design studio where I expanded my skillset.

Education

I studied computer science with an emphasis in software engineering at California State University, Fullerton. While balancing full-time school and a full-time job for the second half of my academic career I was able to excel and graduate in 4.5 years. I also participated in clubs such as Cru, Video Game Development Club, and Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE) along the way.

Passions

My passion is to work with extremely talented people and collaborate to make products that make an impact. I also enjoy solving difficult challenges and learning from them. While at the design studio, I gained a fondness for web. Alongside hard work came knowledge in WordPress development to skillfully handcraft websites.
